Understanding Scottish Fold Cats

Scottish Fold cats are known for their distinctive folded ears and sweet nature. They look like adorable plush toys come to life!

Characteristics

  • Distinctive folded ears with a unique fold pattern
  • Round face with large, expressive eyes
  • Medium-sized with a sturdy build
  • Affectionate, playful, and social - they get along well with dogs and children
